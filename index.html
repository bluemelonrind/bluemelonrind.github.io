About

overview

I am a researcher working on generative models for CAD geometry. My research explores how deep learning models can generate structured, valid, and editable boundary representations.

Recently, I have been working on diffusion-based B-rep generation and NURBS representation learning. I am especially interested in improving NURBS legality, watertightness, and post-processing efficiency in CAD generation pipelines.

Research Interests

what I study

B-rep Generation

Topology-aware generation of CAD boundary representations, including vertices, edges, faces, loops, and solid structures.

NURBS Representation Learning

Learning structured NURBS parameters such as control points, weights, knot vectors, masks, and valid parameter lengths.

Geometric Validity

Improving legality, watertightness, and reconstruction robustness with explicit constraints and OpenCascade-based validation.

AI for CAD

Applying diffusion models, VAEs, and representation learning to engineering design, CAD reconstruction, and shape synthesis.
